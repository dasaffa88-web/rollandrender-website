2. Core Principle: Local Data Storage

Bookielist is fully usable offline. All personal library data is stored exclusively on your device.

Ownership status, reading status, and notes do not leave your device automatically.

3. What data is stored?

  • Book data: title, author, ISBN/EAN, cover URL, publisher (if available)
  • Series data: series name and volume number (if available or added by you)
  • Status data: ownership status (owned / wishlist), reading status (unread / started / finished / stopped)
  • Metadata: timestamps for creation and last update
  • Custom cover images: cover photos you capture or select yourself when you explicitly set them
  • App settings: selected mood mode

The data is stored locally on your device.

4. Online Search and Cover Requests

When using online search or automatic metadata lookup, requests may be sent to external APIs (Google Books API, Open Library API).

Only the search term is transmitted, for example a title or ISBN, not your user profile.

External privacy information:
Google Privacy Policy
Open Library / Internet Archive

When loading external cover images, your IP address may technically be transmitted to the respective image server.

If you use your own cover photo for a book instead, that image remains exclusively on your device and is not transferred to Roll & Render Studio, the community cache, or external book sources.

5. Bookielist Community Cache and Optional Series Sharing

Bookielist uses an optional anonymous community metadata cache (Cloudflare Workers / KV) to speed up matches.

If you fill in missing series information in the book details, you can also share the series name and volume number anonymously with the community. This only happens when you actively trigger that share action.

What is transmitted?

  • ISBN/EAN
  • Book title
  • Author
  • Cover URL
  • Optional: series name and volume number if you actively share them with the community
  • Source of the data, for example Google Books, Open Library, or manual entry

What is not transmitted?

  • Name, username, or account data
  • Ownership status or reading status
  • Notes or other personal library content
  • Self-captured cover photos
  • Personal activity logs
  • Device identifiers generated by the application

Hosting: Cloudflare, Inc. (USA). Cloudflare is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework. More information: Cloudflare Privacy Policy

Legal basis for users in the EEA: Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in faster metadata recognition). Where you actively share series information with the community, that transfer also happens as a direct result of your deliberate user action.

6. Backup and Export (Import / Export)

  • Export only happens on user initiative
  • The storage location is chosen by the user
  • No automatic cloud transfer
  • Import only reads files that are provided locally

Bookielist does not transfer personal backup data to its own cloud.

7. Purchases via Google Play Billing

Purchases of the full version are processed via Google Play Billing. Google may process its own data for this purpose, including your Google account and payment information, in accordance with Google's own privacy policies and terms.

Roll & Render Studio does not view, process, or store full payment details.

In order for the app to recognize whether the full version is unlocked, the purchase status is stored locally on your device.

8. No User Accounts

Bookielist does not require registration, an account, or an email address to use the app.

9. No Tracking, No Advertising

  • No analytics tracking inside the app
  • No advertisements
  • No marketing SDKs or profiling
  • No hidden follow-up costs after the initial purchase

10. Camera Permission (Barcode Scanner)

The camera is only used when you actively open the scanner. No photos or videos are stored.

11. Deletion of Data

You can remove data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual books inside the app
  • Clearing the app storage in Android
  • Uninstalling the app (removes local data)

Note on the cloud cache: it does not contain user-related records, so no person-specific deletion is required there.

12. Your Rights

You may have rights of access, rectification, erasure, and data portability under applicable law.

Contact for privacy-related requests: contact@rollandrender.studio
